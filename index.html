NADEEM SARWAR is a Senior Member of IEEE and working as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science, Bahria University, Lahore, Pakistan. He has 12 years of teaching and research experience. He has published more than 65 international and National Journal/conference publications in the last five years. He is also working as an Editorial Board Member for various research journals named CMC- Computers, Materials & Continua, Applied Computational Intelligence and Soft Computing, PLOS ONE, IET Software, Security and Communication Networks, International Journal of Telemedicine and Applications, Journal of Healthcare Engineering, Milestone transactions on medical Technometrics, Mathematics and Computer Science, SCIREA Journal of Computer. Worked for more than 25 journals as and reviewer and PC member. His research interests are smart healthcare, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Machine learning, Data Science, Biological Computing, IoT, Cloud computing, Wireless Sensor Networks, Cyber security, Image Processing, Software Engineering, Precision in Agriculture.
